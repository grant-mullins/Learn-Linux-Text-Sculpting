Challenge 1: path.txt
Produce a text file containing the PATH variable without any subdirectories of '/var/opt' (including /var/opt):
	/usr/local/sbin:/usr/local/bin:/home/ops/projects/monitor:
	...

Challenge 2: contacts.csv

	name, email, phone
	Alice Johnson, alice.johnson@example.com, 555-1001
	Bob Smith, bsmith@example.com, 555-1002
	Carol Davis, caroldavis3@example.net, 555-1003
	...

Challenge 3: web.log
Produce a log file containing only the lines with a 4xx or 5xx status code, reformat the time, remove the word "for", and prepend "CLIENT ERROR:" and "SERVER ERROR:" to lines with 4xx and 5xx status codes, respectively:
	CLIENT ERROR: 2025-12-01T10:15:40Z 404 api.example.com/foo 10.0.0.1
	SERVER ERROR: 2025-12-01T10:15:48Z 500 admin.example.com/login 10.0.0.9
	SERVER ERROR: 2025-12-01T10:16:04Z 503 api.example.com/vi/items 10.0.0.3
	...

Challenge 5: math.txt
Produce a text file with all numbers (1-digit and 2-digit), operators (+, -, *, /), and equal signs followed by exactly one space (except the last character of each line):
	4 + 5 = 9
	8 - 7 = 1
	10 * 3 = 30
	9 / 3 = 3
	10 + 1 * 5 = 15
	...
